### Controls:

- **Left Click** - Shoot nail gun
- **ESC** - Pause menu

### Gameplay Loop:

1. **Find the Power Cell** (glowing blue object)
   - Press **E** to pick it up

2. **Insert into Power Bay**
   - Look for the power bay station
   - Press **F** when prompted
   - **Check the top of the power bay for inspection results!**

3. **Follow the Lights**
   - Factory lights will turn on sequentially
   - Follow the glowing path

4. **Activate the Console**
   - Find the main factory console
   - Press **F** to activate
   - **Inspection results appear at the top of the console!**

5. **Complete!**
   - View your speedrun time
   - Try to beat your record!

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting:

**Can't pick up power cell?**
- Make sure you're close enough
- Look directly at it
- Press **E** (not F)

**Can't interact with power bay?**
- You must be holding the power cell first
- Stand close to the power bay
- Press **F** when "Press F" appears

**Lost in the factory?**
- Check your minimap (top-right corner)
- Follow the glowing floor lights
- Objective markers show your next goal

**Enemies too difficult?**
- Use cover and corners
- Shoot while moving
- Headshots deal more damage
